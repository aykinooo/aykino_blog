Who am I?

Cybersecurity Student

Hi! I am a cybersecurity enthusiast based in France. Pentester by day, CTF player by night. This blog is my space to share research, writeups, and technical monitoring.

My journey led me to explore multiple areas of security, from web and network pentesting to malware analysis, OSINT, and incident response. I like understanding how systems work and how they can be abused.

When I am not in front of a terminal, I am usually solving CTF challenges, contributing to open-source projects, or reading threat intelligence content.
